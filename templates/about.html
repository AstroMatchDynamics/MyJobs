.JOBS

The .JOBS platform is a Top Level Domain (TLD) that provides an interactive, community-driven network of job related sites.

  • Direct connection to hundreds of thousands of jobs from companies like IBM, Conoco Phillips, Newell Rubbermaid, AT&T and hundreds of other large and small organizations.
  • Direct connection to federal, state and local government agencies, educational institutions and non-profit organizations jobs are available.
  • New jobs are added daily with special emphasis on veterans and military, people with disabilities, diversity, green jobs and many others.
  • Share and Follow tabs make it a seamless and intuitive way to utilize social networking within your job search on the .JOBS platform.
  • Many unique personalization tools offer an enhanced experience for all visitors.

("my.jobs") is a member of the .jobs Universe network of websites. DirectEmployers Association (the "Association") respects the privacy of every individual who visits the .jobs Universe or interacts with us in any way. This Privacy Policy states how the Association will collect information and use it appropriately.

Personal Information

There are several opportunities on our web site for you to provide us with personal information about you and your interests, such as name, mailing address and e-mail address. If you choose to share any personal information with us, we may store it and use it for internal marketing research and other marketing purposes. We will not sell or in any way distribute your personal information to other organizations.

Non-Personal Information

Sometimes, we may collect non-personal information about you that is anonymous data. The information we receive is for the Association’s use only and is not disclosed to any other party except in aggregate form. Examples of this information include the length and time of your visit, the pages you looked at on our site, the type of Internet Browser you have and the site you visited just before coming to ours. We use this information to develop ideas for improving our site and to measure site activity.

Cookies

Our web site makes use of 'cookie' files to customize information to your personal tastes. A cookie is an element of data that a web site can send to your Browser, which may store some information on your computer. When you come back to the .jobs Universe, we can tailor information to suit your individual preferences. The goal is to save you time and provide you with a more meaningful visit. Most Internet Browsers will allow you to erase cookies from your computer hard drive, block all cookies or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. Please refer to your Browser instruction.

Surveys

We request your input to evaluate potential features and services for the Association. The decision to participate or not is completely yours. Also, we might e-mail you a survey, but your involvement is completely voluntary. We do not employ special tools or try to find any e-mail addresses that are not provided to us by you.

.jobs Universe Links

The Association may establish relationships with reputable business partners that allow visitors to the .jobs Universe Network to link directly to sites operated by these partners. We choose our business partners carefully. However, the Association is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of third party web sites that may be linked to the .jobs Universe Network. These links are meant for the user's convenience only and do not constitute sponsorship, endorsement or approval by the Association of the content, policies or practices of such sites or any products or services which may be offered through such sites. Once you have left the .jobs Universe Network via such links, you should check the applicable privacy policy of the third party site.

Children

The Association has no intention of collecting any personal information from individuals under 18 years of age. If a child has provided us with personal information without the consent of a parent or guardian, a parent or guardian should contact us at: info@DirectEmployers.org, if you would like us to delete this information from our files.

Contact Information

Daily operations of the .jobs Universe Network are managed by DirectEmployers Association in Alliance with Employ Media LLC. If you have questions, comments, or need additional information about the Association’s privacy policy or practices, please contact us via email at: info@DirectEmployers.org. You may also contact the Association toll-free at 1-866-268-6206, or via U.S. mail at: DirectEmployers Association, 9002 N. Purdue Road, Suite 100, Indianapolis, IN 46268.
